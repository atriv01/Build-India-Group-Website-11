About Us

Build India Group is a non-profit organization dedicated to nurturing responsible citizenship among India's youth.

Our Mission

Build India Group works with young people across the nation to instill values of good citizenry, environmental consciousness, and national pride.

Through our various initiatives, we aim to create a generation of Indians who are not only proud of their heritage but also actively contribute to the nation's development.

Our Key Initiatives

Discover the programs that form the core of our mission to build a better India.

Pledge Festival

An annual celebration where youth from across India take pledges for good citizenry in 22 languages, promoting unity in diversity.

Aikyatan

Fostering harmony and unity among diverse communities through cultural exchanges and collaborative projects.

Haritbol

Our green initiative focused on environmental conservation, tree plantation, and promoting sustainable practices.

Pledge in 22 Languages

Take the citizen's pledge in your preferred language and join the movement for a better India.

I pledge to be a responsible citizen of India, to respect its constitution, to uphold its democratic values, and to contribute positively to its growth and development. I will work towards building an inclusive, harmonious, and prosperous nation for all.

Our Founder

Meet Biraja Mahapatra, the visionary behind Build India Group.

Biraja Mahapatra

Biraja Mahapatra is a passionate advocate for youth development and nation-building. His vision for Build India Group stems from a deep belief in the potential of India's youth to drive positive change.

In his book "My Call", Biraja describes his dream of an India where every citizen actively contributes to the nation's progress through responsible citizenship.
