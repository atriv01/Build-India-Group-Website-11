About Us

Build India Group is a non-profit organization dedicated to nurturing responsible citizenship among India's youth.

Our Mission

Build India Group works with young people across the nation to instill values of good citizenry, environmental consciousness, and national pride.

Through our various initiatives, we aim to create a generation of Indians who are not only proud of their heritage but also actively contribute to the nation's development.

Our Founder

Meet Biraja Mahapatra, the visionary behind Build India Group.

Biraja Mahapatra

Biraja Mahapatra is a passionate advocate for youth development and nation-building. His vision for Build India Group stems from a deep belief in the potential of India's youth to drive positive change.

In his book "My Call", Biraja describes his dream of an India where every citizen actively contributes to the nation's progress through responsible citizenship.
