Our Story

How Build India Group began its journey to transform youth into responsible citizens

Build India Group was founded in 2010 with a vision to create a movement of responsible citizenship among India's youth. Our founder, Biraja Mahapatra, recognized the need for a platform that would inspire young Indians to actively participate in nation-building.

What started as a small initiative with a handful of volunteers has now grown into a nationwide movement with thousands of participants across 22 states.

Our approach is simple yet powerful: we believe that every individual has the potential to contribute to society, and through our programs, we provide the platform and guidance for them to do so.

Our Mission

To foster a generation of responsible citizens who actively contribute to India's development through ethical practices, environmental consciousness, and inclusive growth.

Our Vision

To create an India where every citizen, especially the youth, takes pride in their national identity and works collaboratively towards a prosperous, harmonious nation.

Our Values

Integrity, Inclusivity, Sustainability, and Patriotism form the core values that guide all our initiatives and interactions.

Our Key Initiatives

Detailed look at the programs that drive our mission forward

Pledge Festival

The Pledge Festival is our flagship event that brings together youth from across India to take the citizen's pledge in 22 languages. This annual celebration promotes unity in diversity and reinforces our commitment to the nation.

Through workshops, cultural exchanges, and community service activities, participants develop a deeper understanding of their role as responsible citizens.

Learn More About Pledges

Aikyatan

Aikyatan, meaning "harmony" in Sanskrit, focuses on fostering unity among India's diverse communities. Through cultural exchanges, collaborative projects, and dialogue sessions, we break down barriers and build bridges of understanding.

This initiative celebrates India's rich cultural heritage while promoting the idea that our diversity is our greatest strength.

Haritbol

Haritbol is our environmental initiative that combines awareness, action, and advocacy for a greener India. We organize tree plantation drives, waste management workshops, and campaigns to promote sustainable living.

Through Haritbol, we aim to create environmentally conscious citizens who understand their responsibility towards preserving our planet for future generations.
